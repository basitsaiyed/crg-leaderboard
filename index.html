This is the tab at the bottom of your Google Sheet — the one that contains all the monthly meeting data.
📋 How the sheet is read:
The app reads your CRG-format sheet automatically. Each month block starts with a title row ("CRG Toastmasters... [Month]"), followed by a CRG Team row, then a header row (TMOD, Prepared Speech - 1, etc.), then meeting rows where Column A = date and the rest = member names.

Column headers supported: TMOD · Prepared Speech - 1/2/3 · Speech Evaluator - 1/2/3 · General Evaluator · Timer · Ah-counter · Grammarian · TTM
